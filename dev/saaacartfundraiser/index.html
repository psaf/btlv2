How It Works

We are a 50/50 art fundraiser. 50% of proceeds go to the artist and 50% of proceeds go to SAAAC Creative. See FAQ for more information.

  1. Pick The Artwork

    Select your favourite artwork by our featured artist or SAAAC Clients.

  2. Setup Payment

    Contact us to setup a convenient method of payment.

  3. Arrange Pickup/Shipping

    Get the artwork in your hands and enjoy!

Who We Are

Behind the Line and South Asian Autism Awareness Centre are teaming up with emerging and professional Canadian artists to raise funds for SAAAC Creative: an art program supporting individuals with Autism

The Artists

Featured Artist

Amir Akbari

Amir Akbari is a visual artist, youth developer and social entrepreneur working with diverse communities across the Greater Toronto Area in various creative capacities. Partnering with numerous agencies and organizations to develop community art projects, special education programs, and settlement initiatives. Amir is an avid painter, drama instructor and educational facilitator with a passion for working with people and igniting inspiration. He founded Behind the Line Creative Social Solutions in 2012 to meet the growing demand of creating more inclusive, creative, and innovative social initiatives. As a graduate of the OCAD University in 2007 Amir hopes to inject an artistic and creative flair wherever he ventures.

The Art

FAQ

  1. Charity

    50% of all proceeds from these featured original artworks will go directly to SAAAC Creative to support clients’ artistic expression and potential.

  2. Tax Receipt

    A tax receipt will be provided by SAAAC for 50% of the total sale price.

  3. Original Artworks

    All artworks are originals and NOT re-prints or duplications.

  4. Payment

    Payment can be made in cash, cheque, or money transfer directly to SAAAC. A deposit may be required if the transaction cannot be completed within one week.

  5. Shipping

    Arrangements for shipping and pick up can be made directly through SAAAC Creative. Shipping and handling will be subject to additional fees.

  6. Pick Up

    Artwork can be picked up at SAAAC Creative. See contact section for address

  7. Framing

    Framing will NOT be provided for any of the artwork.

  8. Recognition

    Special recognition and thanks will be featured on the SAAAC website at the discretion of the purchaser.
