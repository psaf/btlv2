How It Works

  • 50% of all proceedds from these featured original artworks will go directly to SAAAC Creative to support clients' artistic expression and potential.
  • A tax receipt will be provided by SAAAC for 50% of the total sale price.
  • All artworks are originals and NOT re-prints or duplications.
  • Payment can be made in cash, cheque, or money transfer directly to SAAAC.
  • Artwork can be picked up at SAAAC. See below for address.
  • Alternate arrangements for shipping and pick up can be made directly through SAAAC. Shipping may be subject to additional fees.
  • Framing will NOT be provided for any of the artwork.
  • Special recognition and thanks will be featured on the SAAAC website at the descretion of the purchaser.
