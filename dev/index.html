Behind the Line is a social entrepreneurship dedicated to the development and support of social and cultural enhancement through creativity and innovation.

We specialize in designing creative programs to address the developmental growth of individuals with special needs. Programs are facilitated and dedicated to engage individuals with a wide range of mental health issues including but not limited to Autism Spectrum Disorders, psychological disorders, developmental and physical disabilities. The unique and interactive strategies of Behind the Line augment traditional clinical practices that enhance participants’ engagement and healthy development.

Behind the Line and South Asian Autism Awareness Centre are partnering up with talented and professional Canadian artists to raise funds for the SAAAC Creative: an art program supporting individuals with autism.

SAAAC Creative is a visual arts initiative that aims to inspire creativity, enhance cognitive abilities and promote social inclusion through artistic exploration and professional skill development. Our participants work towards creating vibrant works of art to change perceptions, raise awareness and beautify our communities. Our main objective is to promote public awareness of the creative capacity and potential of individuals with Autism, while enhancing participants’ sense of social inclusion through artistic recognition.
